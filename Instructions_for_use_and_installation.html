Instructions for Use & Installation

Battery Installation

Step 1: Open the Battery Compartment

Locate the battery compartment on the device. Open it by sliding or lifting the cover depending on your model.

Step 2: Insert the Battery

Ensure the battery is inserted with the correct polarity. The "+" (positive) side should align with the marked side in the compartment.

Step 3: Close the Battery Compartment

Once the battery is in place, securely close the compartment by sliding or snapping the cover back in place.

Usage Instructions

Step 1: Power On

Press the power button to turn on the device. Wait for the indicator light to turn on, signaling that the device is ready for use.

Step 2: Adjust Settings

Use the control buttons or app interface to adjust settings, including brightness, mode, or other features as per your needs.

Step 3: Monitoring Battery Status

Monitor the battery indicator regularly. If the indicator shows low battery, recharge or replace the battery as needed.

Important Notes

Note: Always use the recommended battery type to ensure optimal performance and avoid damage to the device.

Note: If the device is not in use for extended periods, it is recommended to remove the battery to prevent leakage.
