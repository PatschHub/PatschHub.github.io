Battery Dismantling Information

Safety Precautions:

  • Ensure the battery is fully discharged before dismantling to prevent electric shock.
  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) such as gloves, goggles, and protective clothing.
  • Work in a well-ventilated area to avoid inhaling toxic gases that may be released during dismantling.

Recommended Dismantling Steps:

  • Step 1: Disconnect the battery from any power source.
  • Step 2: Use non-conductive tools to open the battery housing carefully.
  • Step 3: Remove the cells from the housing, ensuring no short-circuits occur.
  • Step 4: Handle the cells with care, and avoid puncturing or damaging them.
  • Step 5: Separate different materials (e.g., plastic, metal, cells) for proper recycling.

Disposal and Recycling:

  • Ensure all dismantled parts are sorted and disposed of according to local recycling and hazardous waste disposal guidelines.
  • Do not discard battery cells or electronics in regular trash. Use designated recycling centers.
