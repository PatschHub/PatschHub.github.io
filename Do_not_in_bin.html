Symbol Explanation - Do Not Dispose in Regular Bin

Symbol: WEEE (Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment Directive)

What Does This Symbol Mean?

This symbol, a crossed-out wheelie bin, indicates that the battery or electronic product should not be disposed of in regular household waste.

Why Is This Important?

  • Environmental Protection: Batteries contain harmful substances that can damage the environment if not disposed of correctly.
  • Recycling & Recovery: Proper disposal allows valuable materials to be recovered and reused.
  • Legal Compliance: Many countries have regulations requiring separate disposal of batteries and electronic waste.

How Should Batteries Be Disposed?

To properly dispose of your battery, take it to a designated recycling facility, battery collection point, or hazardous waste center.

Note: Always check your local regulations for battery disposal and recycling requirements.
