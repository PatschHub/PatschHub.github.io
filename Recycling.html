Symbol Explanation - Recycling Symbol

Symbol: Recycling (Mobius Loop)

What Does This Symbol Mean?

The recycling symbol, also known as the MÃ¶bius loop, indicates that the product or its packaging is recyclable.

Why Is This Important?

  • Environmental Protection: Promotes the reuse of materials and reduces waste.
  • Resource Conservation: Helps in saving raw materials by reprocessing used products.
  • Proper Disposal: Encourages responsible recycling behavior.

How Should Batteries Be Recycled?

Batteries should be disposed of at designated battery collection points or recycling facilities to ensure safe handling and material recovery.

Note: Not all products with this symbol are made from recycled materials. Always check additional information on the packaging.
